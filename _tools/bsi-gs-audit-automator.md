A cloud-native audit automation pipeline for BSI Grundschutz audits that uses the BSI OSCAL catalog of Edition 2023 together with customer documentation to generate evidence-based audit plans, control checks, findings, and report drafts. It extracts and normalizes Grundschutz-Check content into reusable JSON, then enriches the audit workflow by looking up required controls from the BSI OSCAL catalog and combining them with implementation evidence for auditor review.
